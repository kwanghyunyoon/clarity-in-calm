Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Clarity in Calm ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mindfulness and wellness app. Your privacy is fundamental to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Clarity in Calm stores all data locally on your device only. We do not operate servers, collect analytics, or transmit any personal data.

  • Journal entries – mood rating and any text you write. Encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a key stored in the device Keychain / Keystore. Never leaves your device.
  • Breathing sessions – session count. Encrypted and stored locally.
  • Language preference – your chosen language. Stored in device storage.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any account information.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting services.
  • We do not access your microphone, camera, contacts, or location.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Journal Encryption

On iOS and Android, journal entries are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. The encryption key is stored in the device Keychain (iOS) or Keystore (Android) and never leaves your device. On web, the key is stored as a non-extractable CryptoKey in IndexedDB.

Mental Health Disclaimer

Clarity in Calm is a wellness tool, not a medical device or mental health treatment. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a qualified professional or crisis helpline. The in-app crisis resources link to external services; their own privacy policies apply.

Permissions

  • The app requests no OS permissions beyond standard storage access.
  • Camera and photo library permissions listed in the app manifest are not actively used by the current version.

Children

Clarity in Calm does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. All data stays on-device.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
